App Description

Bro IPTV Player is a fast, private, and beautifully simple way to watch M3U playlists on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV.

Add a playlist URL and start playing in seconds - no account, no registration.

Features

  • Works across iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and tvOS
  • Supports M3U and M3U8 playlists
  • Supports EPG
  • Protect playlists with a PIN for extra privacy
  • No account or registration required
  • Share playlists with other devices
  • Clean, fast interface designed for everyday use
