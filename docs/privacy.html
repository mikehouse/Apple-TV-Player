Privacy Policy

Bro IPTV Player

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Bro IPTV Player is an M3U and M3U8 playlist player for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV. The app does not require account registration and does not include built-in channels, media, or playlists.

Information You Provide

  • You may add playlist URLs, optional EPG URLs, optional image/logo URLs, or import playlist files.
  • This information is used only to load, parse, and display content requested by you.
  • If you enable PIN protection, playlist data stored on the device may be encrypted locally.

Information Stored on Your Device

  • Saved playlists and related metadata.
  • App settings and playback-related preferences.
  • Cached playlist images and program guide data when needed for app features.

Information Sent Off Device

  • User-provided playlist, EPG, and image/logo URLs are requested over the network only to fetch the content you ask the app to display.
  • Firebase Analytics is used to understand app usage and installations.
  • Firebase Crashlytics is used to receive crash reports and diagnostics that help improve app stability.

How Data Is Used

  • To load and display user-provided playlists and streams.
  • To provide features such as playlist management, playback, EPG, and logos.
  • To measure app usage at an aggregate level and understand product behavior.
  • To diagnose crashes, errors, and stability problems.

Advertising and Tracking

  • The app does not display third-party ads.
  • The app does not use your data for cross-app or cross-website tracking.
  • The app does not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

Your Choices

  • You can remove playlists from the app at any time.
  • You can choose whether to add or import any playlist, EPG, or image source.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and remove local app data from your device.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact the developer through the project repository: GitHub Issues.

This policy applies to the Bro IPTV Player app distributed through Apple platforms. If the app’s data practices change, this policy will be updated.
